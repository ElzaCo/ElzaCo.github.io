No Future

A motto for the pragmatic optimist.

What kind of company would have "No future" as their motto? It seems rather pessimistic, but it is meant to breath optimism. There is no future, except the one we make for ourselves. We need to be the force for good we desire to see in the world. The world can be a better place, and it will be because of us -- all of us.

Elza Co produces software and hardware tools that are open source. We believe that a tool being inaccessible because of cost disproportionately hurts those with the greatest need. Accordingly, we publish all of our software for free on our site or github as well as the schematics and build files for hardware. We do sell hardware because it saves to buy PCBs and components in bulk, but in the end we pass the savings on to the consumer.

We at Elza Co also consult on geospatial projects. We have expertise in a variety of tasks including: data collection, data processing and analysis, database development, remote drone piloting, and web map application development. If you have a geospatial project, we'd love to hear about it. Contact us in the form below or email us at info@elza-co.com.
